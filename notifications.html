Notifications

Notifications are used to display static information that is relevant to all users of the site, such as scheduled site maintenance or a company closure. For maximum readibility across all devices, notifications should be no more than 140 characters in length and the most important information should be in bold.

Primary notification

There should only be one primary notification on a page. It should be placed before any other page content, just after the main navigation.

On Saturday, May 14, Premera.com will be unavailable starting at 4 p.m. Pacific time for planned maintenance. We expect the work to take 8 hours. We appreciate your patience while we work hard to make our site better!
