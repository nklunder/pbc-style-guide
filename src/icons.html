Iconography

What is an icon?

An icon is a graphical representation of an application that, when clicked on, will trigger a particular action. Generally, icons represent objects, tools, actions or desired results.

Icon usage

The overuse of icons can increase cognitive load for users. The more icons you use, the less attention they draw. Consider whether adding an icon to the interface increases usability and clarity. Use them when space requires it, to communicate status, or to help differentiate object.

Design principles

Shapes are bold and geometric. Symmetry and consistency of shapes give the icons a unique quality, while keeping them simple and bold. Icons can be used in the web, Android, iOS and WP applications. The design is inspired by Google Material Design.
